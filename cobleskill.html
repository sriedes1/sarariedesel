Renewable Energy

Research

The Center for Environmental Science and Technology (CEST) is a center for research and education for SUNY Cobleskill. They research bioenergy and the ways to capture energy released during composting. CEST provides students with the knowledge for: environmental careers, sustaining a healthy environment, and making positive environmental changes in the community and campus. CEST focuses on education, training, and applied research. Their focuses are waste reduction, sustainable soil and water management, the use of syngas as a substitution for diesel fuel, and the use of bioenergy crops, and a little bit of renewable energy technologies.

Renewable Energy Credits

As of 2015, SUNY Cobleskill is not purchasing Renewable Energy Credits nor are they producing enough renewable energy to create their own Renewable Energy Credits. If you would like to know more about Cobleskill or would like to encourage them to buy RECs/ produce more renewable energy, please contact them at admissions@cobleskill.edu or visit Cobleskill.edu
