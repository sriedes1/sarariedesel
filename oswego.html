State University College at Oswego

Renewable Energy

SUNY Oswego has renovated the Sciences Complex and the School of Education to add a geothermal system and a solar panel system. They are also exploring the idea of wind turbines and adding more renewable resources in general. The University has set a goal to be carbon neutral by 2050 and to make a 40% emission reduction of their 2007 rates by 2017.

Research

There is some sustainable research occurring on the Oswego campus, however their efforts are focused much more in different areas. Some of the environmental research includes geese migration from weather changed and how lava flows.

Renewable Energy Credits

There has been discussion as to whether or not the University will participate in carbon offsetting, but no action has been taken yet. Also, as of 2015, SUNY Oswego is not purchasing Renewable Energy Credits nor are they producing enough renewable energy to create their own Renewable Energy Credits. If you would like to know more about Oswego or would like to encourage them to buy RECs/ produce more renewable energy, please contact them at admiss@oswego.edu or visit oswego.edu
